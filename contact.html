Good to know

Before your visit

Do you offer free consultations?

Yes. A 30-minute consultation is complimentary, and so is a 15-minute follow-up appointment. It is the best way to build a plan for your skin.

Do you offer payment plans?

Yes. Buy now, pay later is available in-person for bookings from $50 to $4,000, and you can get approved before your appointment.

What is your cancellation policy?

We ask for at least 48 hours' notice to change or cancel. Cancellations inside 48 hours or no-shows are charged a 20% fee to the card on file.

Will injectables look overdone?

No. Our approach is natural, subtle enhancement tailored to your face, so you look like a rested version of yourself. Every plan is set at your consultation.
